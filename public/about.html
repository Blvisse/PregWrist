About PregWrist

What we aim to Do

We are the world's first pregnancy Monitoring system

PregWrist focuses on how early detection of an increase in pulse rate (tachycardia) can be crucial in the discernment of possible onset of infection, anaemia or haemorrhage in expectant women; thus reducing Maternal deaths. This is achieved by the use of an IoT based pulse rate sensor to relay telemetry to doctors remotely, the data collected will then be run through a machine model that aims to predict chances of any infection, anemia, and early signs of antepartum haemorrhage.
